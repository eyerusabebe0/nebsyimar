About Nefsyimar

Ethiopia's trusted digital sanctuary for honoring the deceased

Nefsyimar is Ethiopia's first digital memorial platform, designed to honor the deceased, connect communities, and preserve memories with dignity and compassion. We provide a sacred digital space where families and friends can come together to celebrate lives and keep memories alive forever.

Compassionate

Built with deep respect for Ethiopian cultural traditions and grieving practices

Community

Connecting families, friends, and communities in times of remembrance

Accessible

Available in multiple Ethiopian languages with culturally appropriate features
